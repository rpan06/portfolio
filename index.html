About Me

I graduated from UCI with a double major in pharmaceutical sciences and film arts. Juggling these two radically different degrees taught me how to manage my time efficiently so that I could graduate in four years. After graduation, Hand and Nail Harmony offered me an incredible design job that I decided to stay with instead of pursuing Pharmacy.

Fortunately, my passion led to a promotion at the company to web designer. Here, I was given the chance to design full web pages, which I would send to the company programmer. This started my interest in website development and I made the hard decision to leave the company to devote all my time to learning programming. It’s one of the best choices I’ve ever made! I’m absolutely thrilled to be in a career that’s never stagnant, where you are constantly learning and trying to better develop your skills. It's so cool to work on a team, putting together the little bits of code you wrote to create something amazing!
